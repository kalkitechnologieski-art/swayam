🚀 Getting Started

What is Swayam?
Swayam is a free AI-powered website builder that helps you create a professional website in minutes with zero coding.
How do I start building?
Click "New Site" on your dashboard or visit the Builder to describe your business and let AI generate your site.
Is Swayam really free?
Yes! The core builder, hosting, and 4 themes are completely free forever. Pro plans are optional.

⚙️ Features & Customization

What themes are available?
Veda (Spiritual), Karma (Agency), Sattva (E‑commerce), and Dharma (Consulting) — each production‑ready.
Can I use my own domain?
Yes! You can purchase a domain for ₹3000/year or connect your existing domain to Vercel.
What is Swayam Pro?
Pro gives you a 30‑page Next.js site, international SEO, GEO, AEO, and dedicated support for ₹4,999/month.

🛠️ Technical

Where is my site hosted?
All sites are deployed to Vercel with a free *.vercel.app subdomain and auto‑HTTPS.
Can I download my code?
Yes! You own your code. Download the full HTML/CSS/JS from the Deploy step.
What AI models do you use?
We use Groq (fastest inference) with Zhipu as a fallback for reliability.

💳 Billing & Support

How do I upgrade to Pro?
Visit Billing in your dashboard and select the Pro plan. You'll be redirected to secure payment.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time without penalty. You keep your website.
How do I contact support?
Use this Support Center, create a ticket, or chat with our team live.
