Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Swayam.

Is Swayam really free?
Yes! The core website builder, AI generation, Vercel hosting, and 4 themes are completely free — forever. No credit card required. You only pay if you want to upgrade to Pro or purchase a custom domain.
What do I get with Swayam Pro?
Swayam Pro gives you a 30‑page Next.js site with TypeScript and Tailwind, a full international SEO campaign, GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) for ChatGPT/Perplexity, AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) for voice search, 10 blogs/month, 15 backlinks/month, and a dedicated account manager. All for ₹4,999/month.
Can I use my own domain?
Absolutely. You can purchase a domain directly through Swayam for ₹3,000/year (includes .com, .in, .co, and others). We handle all the DNS configuration automatically. Or you can use your existing domain by pointing it to Vercel.
Do I own the code?
Yes, 100%. You can download the full HTML/CSS/JS source code anytime. With Pro, you get the complete Next.js repository. You are never locked into Swayam — you can host your site anywhere.
How does the AI generation work?
We use Groq (fastest inference) as our primary AI engine, with Zhipu as a reliable fallback. You describe your business in 1‑2 sentences, and our AI generates a complete multi‑page website with SEO metadata, schema, and beautiful design in under 5 seconds.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel at any time with no penalty. You keep your website and all the code. If you cancel, you simply lose access to the ongoing SEO/GEO/AEO services and support.
